What AI can & can't do
Roughly, versus a capable human today. The gap is narrower than the hype and wider than the fear.

✓ Good at

  • Drafting, rewriting & summarizing text
  • Explaining things at any level you ask
  • Translating & changing tone or format
  • Writing and debugging code
  • Brainstorming and first drafts, fast
  • Pulling structure out of messy notes

✗ Weak / unreliable at

  • Knowing what's true — it can sound sure and be wrong
  • Fresh facts after its training cutoff
  • Exact math & counting, without tools
  • Remembering you between chats (by default)
  • Real judgment, ethics, accountability
  • Anything where a confident guess is dangerous
